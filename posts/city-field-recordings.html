Field recordings from the city

Feb 02, 2026 · Field

I spent the morning walking with a small recorder, following the quiet transitions between street corners. The city sounds like layers of breath, metal, and distant voices.

The best textures came from the quiet parts: train brakes in the distance, a low rumble under the bridge, and the soft shiver of leaves against concrete.

I am building a small library of loops and one-shots that will feed the next EP, especially the sections that need movement without rhythm.
