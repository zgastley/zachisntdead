Building a tiny collage zine

Feb 27, 2026 · Process

I want this zine to feel hand-made but intentional. I started with old scans, torn edges, and a single sheet of warm stock that already carries a soft grain.

The layout is simple: one image per spread, a short line of text, and a column of raw notes on the back. The edges get a light wash so the paper shows through.

Next step is printing a small batch on rough recycled stock, then hand-stitching the spine before trimming the edges.
