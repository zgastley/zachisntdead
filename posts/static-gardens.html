Static Gardens: first mix notes

Mar 12, 2026 · Studio

Today’s mix pass finally landed in the pocket. The drums are dry and gritty, the pads are wide and glassy, and the vocal chain has just the right amount of grit without smearing the consonants.

I am leaning into the contrast: tight low-end, airy mids, and tiny bursts of noise tucked under the chorus. The hook feels less like a big moment and more like a slow bloom.

Next pass: carve the mids on the synth stack, add a touch of tape flutter to the bass bus, and print a version that is more raw for a live set test.
