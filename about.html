About the archive

My personal digital records.

Why

I made this archive so the work has a home that isn't controlled by a feed. It is a place for songs, drawings, notes, and half-built ideas to live. It is also a promise to myself to keep making, even when the output is messy or slow.

How it's built

The site is intentionally simple: plain HTML, CSS, and a small bit of JavaScript for theme switching, the heart-rate status, and small interactions. It is lightweight by design so I can keep it up without getting overwhelmed.

Long-term intent

This is meant to be a long, living record. If I stop posting for a while, that gap is also part of the record. In general, I just want a solid, free, landing place for my creatie work.
